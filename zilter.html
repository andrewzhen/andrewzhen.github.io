Description

The goal of this project was to focus on hands-on practice with a modern human-centered design process while building a web application to serve a set of target users. The way we materialized this goal was via an web application concept that aims to ease the shopping experience in its entirety by eliminating the need for users to conduct research on a product.


Zilter’s sole purpose is to enhance the recreational activity of online shopping. Online shopping for entertainment gadgets and appliances (e.g. TVs, stereos, laptops, headphones) can be a tedious task, especially with all the research required to get the best product at the best price. This web app provides the user with all the information they need to make an informed decision: standard product information, photos, videos, and official reviews pulled from external websites. And as always, the sources are cited in a bibliography.

Responsibility

My main role was to implement digital mockups into user testable prototypes and in turn, present these to an audience while delivering a pitch for our concept. I shared the responsibility of performing user testing and turning feedback into thoughtful design tweaks.

Branding

The name was inspired by the physical functionality of a filter. Like a filter, we hope that our web app removes unwanted noise when trying to separate out important and non-important information.


The black and gray color scheme was chosen to reflect the simplicity of our mission goal of making product purchases clear and straightforward. The focus of our site are the products themselves so minimal attention should go towards anything else. The green accents were infused to add a splash of color representing the excitement of making a purchase and also to build brand identity. Furthermore, the black and green combination is reminiscent of old school terminals which brings us to the new age of information that our web app presents. The Zilter logo accentuates the letter Z by shaping it into the silhouette of a lightning bolt which represents fast access to information at one’s fingertips that we hope to provide users in order to make an informed purchase.
