Design Co

Web Development

Description

Design Co is a student-run, pre-professional design organization at UC San Diego. Our goal is to educate the community on design and help prepare them for industry work. We plan and organize weekly events that include talks, information sessions and workshops and we consistently see a turnout of between 50 and a 100 people. At our events, students form lasting connections with each other and are increasingly interested in attending. Our website allows the community to stay informed of these upcoming events and so it is imporant that content remain up to date and accessible.

Responsibility

My role as web developer is directly responsible for delivering content to Design Co’s website. I collaborate with brand and visual designers to integrate graphics and high-fidelity design prototypes onto our online presence and related event pages such as Stride, our design-centric career fair, and Design Frontiers, our day-long design sprint.
