Hi, I'm Andrew.

I was born and raised in the heart of the San Francisco Bay Area in Oakland, though I did spent a couple of years as a toddler stumbling around Jiangmen, China—my parents’ beloved hometown.

Early on, I began to take interest in art and drawing because of my brother, who first took painting lessons. From then on, I was known as “that kid” who drew. Though I never really pursued this artistic interest beyond middle school, I made up in trying plenty of other hobbies and activities.

Today, I love being outdoors and especially playing basketball, practicing photography and visual designs, and computer programming. Meanwhile, attending UC San Diego has marked a significant period of my life. I’ve met many great people and I’ve learned so much about myself and what it means to attend a prestigious university in one of the best geographical places in the world.
