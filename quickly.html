Description

Today, almost every student has their class schedules on Google Calendars. The remaining 2.73% of students choose to make their phone wallpapers as their schedules, which is appalling from a designer’s standpoint.


Yet, there still exists a hindrance with the current setup. Let’s take a look:

The challenge presented here is having to painstakingly enter course details. Students usually submit the title, start and end time, repetition during a week and for how many weeks, location, notification setting and calendar account setting. Then, they repeat for each course.


Quickly is a solution to this labor by cutting down on the number of interactions.
